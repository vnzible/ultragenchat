How to Use NeoChat

1

Create Your Account

Click "Create New Account", choose a username and password. That's all you need!

2

Find Friends

Use the "Add Friend" button in the dashboard to search for other users by their username.

3

Accept Requests

Check the "Friend Requests" section to accept or reject incoming friend requests.

4

Start Chatting

Click on any friend in your friends list to start a real-time conversation.

5

Typing Indicators

See when someone is typing to you, and get read receipts when your messages are seen.
